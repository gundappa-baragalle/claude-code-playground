**[Q3488]** [2024]
> Which one of the following is synthesised in human body that dilates blood vessels and increases blood flow?

  ✅ A) Nitric oxide
     B) Nitrous oxide
     C) Nitrogen dioxide
     D) Nitrogen pentoxide

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may confuse nitric oxide (NO — vasodilator) with nitrous oxide (N₂O — laughing gas/anaesthetic) or nitrogen dioxide (NO₂ — air pollutant).

     - Basis for nitroglycerin (angina treatment) and erectile dysfunction drugs (PDE5 inhibitors)

**[Q2290]** [2009]
> In the context of genetic disorders, consider the following : A woman suffers from colour blindness while her husband does not suffer from it. They have a son and a daughter. In this context, which one of the following statements is most probable) correct ?

     A) Both children suffer from colour blindness.
     B) Daughter suffers from colour blindness while son does not suffer from it.
     C) Both children do not suffer from colour blindness.
  ✅ D) Son suffers from colour blindness while daughter does not suffer from it.

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may think the daughter also gets colour blindness — but the daughter gets one X-chromosome from colour-blind mother (X^c) and one normal X from father (X^N), making her a carrier, not colour blind. The son gets X^c from mother and Y from father, making him colour blind.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Colour blindness is X-linked recessive. Mother (X^c X^c) passes X^c to both children. Father (X^N Y) gives X^N to daughter and Y to son. Son: X^c Y = colour blind. Daughter: X^c X^N = carrier, not colour blind. Answer is D.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Colour blindness is X-linked recessive trait
     - Affected mother (X^cX^c) passes defective allele to all children
     - Sons who get X^c from mother will be colour blind; daughters become carriers

### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- Beadle & Tatum (one gene-one enzyme) vs Jacob & Monod (operon) easily mixed up — both 20th-century molecular biology Nobel laureates.
- CDFD=Hyderabad well-known. But CIMAP=Lucknow (not Delhi) and IMTech=Chandigarh create confusion. NII in New Delhi (not Hyderabad despite biotech hub) is counter-intuitive.
- Students near water may associate insects with gills. 'Simple' looking insects suggest skin respiration. Tracheal system is specific to arthropods and counter-intuitive — no visible breathing organ.
- Manganese and Molybdenum are required in trace (micro) quantities, not major quantities. Students confuse macro-minerals with micronutrients.
- Students may confuse fruit-ovary with seed-stem. The plant biology match: Fruit←Ovary, Seed←Ovule, Wood←Stem, Starch←Leaf (photosynthesis) is a standard biology fact.
