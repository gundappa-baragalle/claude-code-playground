     D) I only

**[Q4741]** [2023]
> Consider the following statements: The 'Stability and Growth Pact' of the European Union is a treaty that  1. limits the levels of the budgetary deficit of the countries of the European Union 2. makes the countries of the European Union to share their infrastructure facilities 3. enables the countries of the European Union to share their technologies  How many of the above statements are correct?

  ✅ A) Only one
     B) Only two
     C) All three
     D) None

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Statements 2 and 3 about infrastructure sharing and technology sharing are not part of the EU's Stability and Growth Pact — the SGP is solely about fiscal discipline.
  💡 **Eliminate:** EU's Stability and Growth Pact (1997) limits budget deficits to 3% of GDP and debt to 60% of GDP — fiscal discipline only. No infrastructure or technology sharing. Only Statement 1 is correct. Answer: A (Only one).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - EU Stability and Growth Pact (SGP) limits member states' budget deficit to 3% of GDP and debt to 60% of GDP
     - SGP enforced through Excessive Deficit Procedure (EDP) under EU Commission
