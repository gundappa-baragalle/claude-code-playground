**[Q1433]** [2004]
> Shirin Ebadi, who won the Nobel Peace Prize in 2003, is from:

     A) Iraq
     B) Nigeria
  ✅ C) Iran
     D) Libya

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Iraq and Libya are also Middle Eastern Muslim-majority countries that students may associate with women's rights activism, causing confusion.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Shirin Ebadi is the first Muslim woman to win Nobel Peace Prize and is an Iranian human rights lawyer; Iran is the correct answer.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Shirin Ebadi — first Muslim woman Nobel Peace Prize winner (2003)
     - She is an Iranian lawyer and human rights activist

**[Q1180]** [2002]
> Who was the first Indian actress to receive the Padma Shri Award ?

     A) Samita Patil
  ✅ B) Nargis Dutt
     C) Meena Kumari
     D) Madhubala

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Meena Kumari and Madhubala are prominent actresses but Nargis Dutt was the first Indian actress to receive Padma Shri (1958). Smita Patil received it later.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Nargis Dutt was the first Indian actress to receive the Padma Shri Award in 1958. She is also known for her social work and was an MP (Rajya Sabha). Answer B.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Nargis Dutt received Padma Shri in 1958 — first Indian actress to receive this honor
     - Nargis was famous for 'Mother India' (1957) and was later elected to Rajya Sabha
