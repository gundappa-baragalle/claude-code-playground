### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- Lata Mangeshkar is the most famous Indian cultural icon and was highly prominent in 2001 (received Bharat Ratna that year), making her a strong distractor for this award in the same year.
- Dilip Kumar, Raj Kapoor and Dev Anand are all legendary actors — but checking whether all three received Dadasaheb Phalke Award requires verification. Dev Anand did not receive it.
- Match-list questions on performing arts require precise pairing; confusing Birju Maharaj (Kathak) with Bharatnatyam or Bhajan Sopori's instrument (Santoor) with others are common errors.
- Candidates may confuse Albert Costa (2002 winner) with Juan Carlos Ferrero (2003 winner) as both are Spanish clay court specialists from the same era.
- Candidates may confuse Amol Palekar (director of 'Paheli') with Sanjay Leela Bhansali who is a more prominent commercial director.
