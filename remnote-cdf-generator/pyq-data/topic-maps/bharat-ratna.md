## Prelims Questions

### 🟡 MEDIUM Priority — Full Options

**[Q3139]** [2021] Consider the following statements in respect of Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards:  1. Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are titles under the Article 18(1) of the Constitution of India. 2. Padma Awards, which were instituted in the year 1954, were suspended only once. 3. The number of Bharat Ratna Awards is restricted to a maximum of five in a particular year.  Which of the above statements are not correct?

     A) 1 and 2 only
     B) 2 and 3 only
     C) 1 and 3 only
  ✅ D) 1, 2 and 3

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may incorrectly assume Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are titles (they are NOT under Article 18(1)); Padma Awards were actually suspended twice, not once; Bharat Ratna has no fixed maximum per year.

**[Q615]** [2001] Which of the following pairs is NOT correctly matched ?

     A) India’s first Technicolour film ... Jhansi Ki Rani
     B) India’s first 3-D film ... My Dear Kuttichat
     C) India’s first insured film ... Taal
  ✅ D) India’s first actress to win the Bharat Ratna ... Meena Kumari

  ⚠️ **Trap:** The question asks for the NOT correctly matched pair; students may not know that Meena Kumari never received the Bharat Ratna (she died in 1972 and was never awarded it); Lata Mangeshkar received it in 2001.

### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- The question asks for the NOT correctly matched pair; students may not know that Meena Kumari never received the Bharat Ratna (she died in 1972 and was never awarded it); Lata Mangeshkar received it in 2001.
- Raj Kapoor is a celebrated Bollywood icon and may seem worthy of Bharat Ratna; students may confuse Dadasaheb Phalke Award recipients with Bharat Ratna awardees.
- Students may incorrectly assume Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are titles (they are NOT under Article 18(1)); Padma Awards were actually suspended twice, not once; Bharat Ratna has no fixed maximum per year.
- Satyajit Ray received Bharat Ratna in 1992 (posthumous). Dr. Abdul Kalam received it in 1997. Homi Bhabha received it in 1966. Candidates may confuse years of award.
