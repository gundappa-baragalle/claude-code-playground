**[Q3523]** [2024]
> What are the duties of the Chief of Defence Staff (CDS) as Head of the Department of Military Affairs ?  1. Permanent Chairman of Chiefs of Staff Committee 2. Exercise military command over the three Service Chiefs 3. Principal Military Advisor to Defence Minister on all tri-service matters  Select the correct answer using the code given below :

     A) 1, 2 and 3
     B) 1 and 2 only
     C) 2 and 3 only
  ✅ D) 1 and 3 only

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Statement 2 says CDS exercises military command over Service Chiefs — this is the biggest trap; CDS is NOT in the chain of command over Service Chiefs operationally.
  💡 **Eliminate:** CDS is Permanent Chairman of COSC (1 correct) and Principal Military Advisor to Defence Minister on tri-service matters (3 correct). CDS does NOT exercise operational command over Service Chiefs (2 wrong). Answer is D (1 and 3).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - CDS created in January 2020 is Permanent Chairman of Chiefs of Staff Committee (COSC)
     - CDS is Principal Military Advisor to Defence Minister on tri-service matters but NOT in operational chain of command over individual Service Chiefs

**[Q3524]** [2024] Operations undertaken by the Army towards upliftment of the local population in remote areas to include addressing of their basic needs is called :

     A) Operation Sankalp
     B) Operation Maitri
  ✅ C) Operation Sadbhavana
     D) Operation Madad

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Operation Maitri (Nepal earthquake relief) and Operation Sankalp (Gulf safety) sound like humanitarian civil operations — Operation Sadbhavana is the specific term for army's civil outreach in J&K and NE.

### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- Armoured Corps Centre and School is in Ahmednagar (Maharashtra), not Jabalpur — Jabalpur is associated with Infantry School, causing confusion.
- Students may confuse the foreign intelligence services of major powers — particularly swapping Russia (SVR) with Britain (MI6/SIS) or China (MSS).
- Pair 1 (Brigadier-Air Commodore-Commander) is incorrect as Army Commander = Navy Commodore, not Commander; Pair 2 incorrect as Major General ≠ Vice Admiral.
- Statement 2 says CDS exercises military command over Service Chiefs — this is the biggest trap; CDS is NOT in the chain of command over Service Chiefs operationally.
- Operation Maitri (Nepal earthquake relief) and Operation Sankalp (Gulf safety) sound like humanitarian civil operations — Operation Sadbhavana is the specific term for army's civil outreach in J&K and NE.
