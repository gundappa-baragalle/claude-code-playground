## Prelims Questions

### 🟡 MEDIUM Priority — Full Options

**[Q151]** [1996]
> Prem Vatika, poems on the life of Krishna, was composed by

     A) Bihari
     B) Surdas
  ✅ C) Raskhan
     D) Kabir

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Surdas wrote extensively on Krishna (Sur Sagar) making him the obvious choice. Raskhan — a Muslim devotee of Krishna — is the correct answer but is lesser-known among UPSC aspirants.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Prem Vatika = Raskhan (Muslim poet, devotee of Krishna, wrote 52 verses). Sur Sagar = Surdas. Bihari Satsai = Bihari. Kabir = Dohas (monotheist, no Krishna-specific devotion). Raskhan's uniqueness: Muslim writing bhakti poetry on Krishna.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Raskhan: Muslim Bhakti poet, devoted to Krishna, wrote Prem Vatika and Sujanranjana
     - Sur Sagar: Surdas (blind poet, disciple of Vallabhacharya)
     - Raskhan: Said 'Maanush hau to wohi Raskhan basio braj gokul gaon ke gwaran'
