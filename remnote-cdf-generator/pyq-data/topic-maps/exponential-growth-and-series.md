**[Q2353]** [2010]
> A man fills a basket with eggs in such a way that the number of eggs added on each successive day is the same as the number already present in the basket. This way the basket gets completely filled in 24 days. After how many days the basket was 1/4thfull ?

     A) 6
     B) 12
     C) 17
  ✅ D) 22

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may divide 24 by 4 to get 6 days (for 1/4 full) — but because the basket doubles each day, working backwards is the correct approach.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Basket doubles daily. If full on day 24, then half-full on day 23, quarter-full on day 22. So basket is 1/4th full after 22 days. Answer: D (22).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Doubling problem: if full = day 24, then 1/2 full = day 23, 1/4 full = day 22 (work backwards)
     - This is exponential growth — each day basket = previous day × 2

**[Q904]** [2001]
> Water is filled in a container in such a manner that its volume doubles after every five minutes. If takes 30 minutes for the container to be full, in how much time will it be one-fourth full ?

     A) 7 minutes and 30 seconds
     B) 10 minutes
  ✅ C) 20 minutes
     D) 25 minutes

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students work forward (1/4 full at 7.5 or 10 min) instead of backward from full. If full at 30 min, half-full at 25 min, quarter-full at 20 min — work backward.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Think backward: full at t=30; halved each step backward means half-full at t=25, quarter-full at t=20. The answer is 20 minutes.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Doubling time: reverse means halving each step back
     - 1/4 = two halvings backward from full
