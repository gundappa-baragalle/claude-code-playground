**[Q4697]** [2024]
> Which one of the following shows a unique relationship with an insect that has coevolved with it and that is the only insect that can pollinate this tree ?

  ✅ A) Fig
     B) Mahua
     C) Sandalwood
     D) Silk cotton

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may choose Mahua or Sandalwood (which have specific associated insects) but Fig is the classic example of obligate mutualism — fig wasps are the only pollinators and cannot survive without fig trees.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Fig tree and fig wasp (Agaonidae family): obligate mutualism — fig wasp pollinates the fig and lays eggs inside; neither can reproduce without the other. Answer: A.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Fig-fig wasp mutualism: fig wasp is sole pollinator of fig; lays eggs inside fig syconium; both species obligatorily dependent
     - This is a classic example of coevolution and obligate mutualism in ecology

**[Q4011]** [2020]
> In the context of India, which of the following is/are considered to be practice(s) of eco-friendly agriculture ?  1. Crop diversification 2. Legume intensification 3. Tensiometer use 4. Vertical farming  Select the correct answer using the code given below:

  ✅ A) 1, 2 and 3 only
     B) 3 only
     C) 4 only
     D) 1, 2, 3 and 4

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Tensiometer use (statement 3) is primarily an irrigation scheduling tool — eco-friendly by optimizing water use. Vertical farming (statement 4) is debatable — it uses artificial lighting and controlled environments which may have high energy inputs. All four are generally considered eco-friendly.
  💡 **Eliminate:** All four — crop diversification (reduces monoculture risks), legume intensification (nitrogen fixation reduces synthetic fertilizer), tensiometer use (optimal irrigation), vertical farming (reduces land use) — are eco-friendly practices. Answer is A (1, 2 and 3 only) per official key, suggesting vertical farming is excluded.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Crop diversification: reduces soil depletion, pest pressure, enhances resilience
     - Legume intensification: biological nitrogen fixation reduces synthetic fertilizer need
     - Tensiometer: measures soil moisture tension to optimize irrigation timing

**[Q2411]** [2011]
> Three of the following criteria have contributed to the recognition of Western Ghats-Sri Lanka and Indo Burma regions as hotspots of biodiversity :  1. Species richness 2. Vegetation density 3. Endemism 4. Ethno-botanical importance 5. Threat perception 6. Adaptation of flora and fauna to warm and humid conditions  Which three of the above are correct criteria in this context?

     A) 1, 2 and 6
     B) 2, 4 and 6
  ✅ C) 1, 3 and 5
     D) 3, 4 and 6

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Vegetation density (statement 2) sounds plausible as a biodiversity criterion but is NOT a standard criterion for hotspot recognition; ethno-botanical importance (statement 4) and adaptation (statement 6) are also distractors not among the three official criteria.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Biodiversity hotspots are defined by species richness, endemism, and threat perception (must have lost 70%+ of original habitat). Eliminate statements 2, 4, and 6 as non-standard criteria to arrive at C (1, 3, 5).
