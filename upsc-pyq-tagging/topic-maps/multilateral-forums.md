**[Q4582]** [2023]

     A) 1 only
     B) 2 only
  ✅ C) Both 1 and 2
     D) Neither 1 nor 2

### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- Statement I contains specific dollar figures (600 million to 5 billion) — these figures are inaccurate/dated. Statement III claims 'triple by 2000' — also was not realized. Only II (agricultural exports) has some validity.
- IMF is frequently confused as a World Bank constituent. The World Bank Group has 5 agencies; IMF is separate. Students often club IMF with World Bank as 'Bretton Woods institutions'.
- Statements III (balanced trade management) and IV (East-West trade promotion) sound plausible as WTO aims but are not its mandated objectives. WTO does NOT manage balance or promote specific geopolitical trade flows.
- Option D (III and IV) is tempting as grants and food aid are visible forms of assistance; candidates may underestimate the role of soft loans and technical assistance.
- Multi-fibre Agreement (MFA) was being phased out under WTO, making C seem plausible. Multilateral Agreement on Investment (B) was a separate OECD-led initiative often confused with WTO outcomes.
