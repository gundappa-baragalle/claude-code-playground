### Common Trap Patterns on This Topic
- Greenhouse effect and ozone depletion are common environmental issues and may seem plausible; El Niño's specific drought-inducing effect on Southeast Asia is less well-known.
- Rio de Janeiro (1992 Earth Summit) is the classic distractor. Stockholm was the 1972 conference. The 1997 climate conference was held in Kyoto, producing the Kyoto Protocol.

- New Zealand (carbon trading scheme) and Germany are both climate-conscious nations that may seem like correct answers; Australia's early carbon tax proposal is less known.
- Students may think wetlands are sinks (absorbing methane) rather than sources. Rice fields are less obvious methane sources compared to coal mines and cattle.
