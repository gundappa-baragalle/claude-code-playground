**[Q903]** [2001]
> A conveyer belt delivers baggage at the rate of 3 tons in 5 minutes, and a second conveyer belt delivers baggage at the rate of 1 ton in 2 minutes. How much time will it take to get 33 tons of baggage delivered using both the conveyer belts ?

     A) 25 minutes and 30 seconds
  ✅ B) 30 minutes
     C) 35 minutes
     D) 40 minutes and 45 seconds

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students add rates incorrectly as 3+1=4 tons per 5+2=7 minutes. Must convert to common unit (per minute) before adding.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Rate1 = 3/5 tons/min = 0.6; Rate2 = 1/2 tons/min = 0.5; Combined = 1.1 tons/min. Time = 33/1.1 = 30 minutes exactly.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Work rate addition: R_total = R1 + R2
     - Always convert to same unit before adding rates
