**[Q1891]** [2007]
> Match List I with List II and select the correct answer using the code given below the Lists (Person) | (Known As) A. Bhajan Sopori | 1. Bharatnatyam dancer B. Birju Maharaj | 2. Exponent of Santoor C. Priyadarsini Govind | 3. Mridangam maestro D. T.V. Gopalakrishnan | 4. Kathak dancer

     A) A-2, B-1, C-4, D-3
  ✅ B) A-3, B-1, C-4, D-2
     C) A-2, B-4, C-1, D-3
     D) A-3, B-4, C-1, D-2

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Match-list questions on performing arts require precise pairing; confusing Birju Maharaj (Kathak) with Bharatnatyam or Bhajan Sopori's instrument (Santoor) with others are common errors.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Birju Maharaj is India's most famous Kathak dancer (4); Bhajan Sopori is Santoor maestro (2); Priyadarsini Govind is Bharatnatyam dancer (1); T.V. Gopalakrishnan is Mridangam maestro (3). Answer: A-2, B-4, C-1, D-3 = Option C.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Birju Maharaj = Kathak (Lucknow Gharana)
     - Bhajan Sopori = Santoor (Kashmiri tradition)
     - T.V. Gopalakrishnan = Mridangam

**[Q1423]** [2004]
> Consider the following statements: 1. Hema Malini is the Chairperson of the Children’s Film Society, India. 2. Yash Chopra is the Chairman of the Central Board of Film Certification of India. 3. Dilip Kumar, Raj Kapoor and Dev Anand have all been recipients of Dada Saheb Phalke Award. Which of the statements given above is/ are correct?

     A) 1 and 3
     B) 2 and 3
     C) 2 only
  ✅ D) 3 only

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Dilip Kumar, Raj Kapoor and Dev Anand are all legendary actors — but checking whether all three received Dadasaheb Phalke Award requires verification. Dev Anand did not receive it.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Dilip Kumar (1994) and Raj Kapoor (1987) received Dadasaheb Phalke Awards but Dev Anand never received it — making Statement 3 false. Only Statement 3 being asked → Option D (3 only if all three received it is wrong).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Dadasaheb Phalke recipients: Dilip Kumar (1994), Raj Kapoor (1987); Dev Anand did NOT receive it
     - Answer D (3 only) implies only Dilip Kumar and Raj Kapoor, not Dev Anand — but Q implies all three

**[Q1198]** [2003]
> Who among the following was selected for the 2001 Dada Saheb Phalke Award?

     A) Dev Anand
     B) Lata Mangeshkar
     C) Ramanand Sagar
  ✅ D) Yash Chopra

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Lata Mangeshkar is the most famous Indian cultural icon and was highly prominent in 2001 (received Bharat Ratna that year), making her a strong distractor for this award in the same year.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Lata Mangeshkar received Bharat Ratna in 2001, NOT Phalke Award (she received Phalke in 1989). Dev Anand received Phalke in 2002. 2001 Phalke Award = Yash Chopra. Year-Award-Person mapping required.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Yash Chopra: Dadasaheb Phalke Award 2001 (49th National Film Awards)
     - Lata Mangeshkar: Dadasaheb Phalke 1989, Bharat Ratna 2001
     - Dadasaheb Phalke: India's highest film honour, given annually

**[Q1314]** [2003]

  ✅ A) Albert Costa
     B) Juan Carlos Ferrero
     C) Andre Agassi
     D) Pete Sampras

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Candidates may confuse Albert Costa (2002 winner) with Juan Carlos Ferrero (2003 winner) as both are Spanish clay court specialists from the same era.

     - French Open is played on clay at Roland Garros, Paris
