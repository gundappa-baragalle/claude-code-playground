  *(~250 words: intro 37w / body 162w / conclusion 50w)*
  - 🔑 **Intro:** The Quad's transformation from a post-tsunami maritime cooperation forum into a multilateral strategic grouping reflects the Indo-Pacific's emergence as the new geopolitical fulcrum, though its characterization as a trade bloc remains contested.
  - 📋 **Body:**
      • Origins: 2004 tsunami relief; revived 2017 as strategic forum; leaders' summit 2021
