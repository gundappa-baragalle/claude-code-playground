**[Q905]** [2001]
> A city has a population of 3,00,000 out of which 1,80,000 are males. 50% of the population is literate. If 70% of the males are literate, the number of literate females is

  ✅ A) 24,000
     B) 30,000
     C) 54,000
     D) 60,000

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Some students compute 50% of 120,000 females directly = 60,000 (treating female literacy rate as 50%) instead of using total literate minus literate males.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Total literate = 50% × 300,000 = 150,000. Literate males = 70% × 180,000 = 126,000. Literate females = 150,000 − 126,000 = 24,000.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Total literate = overall rate × total population
     - Literate females = Total literate − Literate males
