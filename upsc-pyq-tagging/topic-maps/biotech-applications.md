> With reference to monoclonal antibodies, often mentioned in news, consider the following statements :  I. They are man-made proteins. II.      B) II and III only
     C) I and III only
  ✅ D) I, II and III

     - Monoclonal antibodies (mAbs): lab-produced proteins that mimic immune system, bind specific target antigens
     - Used in cancer (Herceptin), autoimmune diseases, COVID-19 (casirivimab+imdevimab), and emerging viruses like Nipah
