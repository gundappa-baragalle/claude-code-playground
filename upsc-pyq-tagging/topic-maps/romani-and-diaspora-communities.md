**[Q2172]** [2009]
> In which one of the following places is the Shompen tribe found ?

     A) Nilgiri Hills
  ✅ B) Nicobar Islands
     C) Spiti Valley
     D) Lakshadweep Islands

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Nilgiri Hills (home to Todas, Badagas) and Lakshadweep might seem plausible for a tribal group, but Shompen are exclusive to the Nicobar Islands.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Shompen is a Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Group (PVTG) found exclusively in the interior of Great Nicobar Island in the Nicobar Islands, Andaman & Nicobar.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Shompen tribe is a PVTG found only on Great Nicobar Island; estimated population under 300
     - Shompen are a nomadic tribe with minimal contact with mainstream society; threatened by development projects on Great Nicobar

**[Q1]** [1995]
> The original home of the gypsies was

     A) Egypt
     B) Russia
  ✅ C) India
     D) Persia

  ⚠️ **Trap:** The word 'gypsy' derives from 'Egyptian' — a common medieval European belief — making Egypt seem correct. Russia is plausible due to large Romani population in Eastern Europe.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Linguistic and genetic studies conclusively place Romani origins in Northwest India (Punjab/Sindh). The Egyptian etymology is a misnomer. Eliminate Egypt (etymology trap) and Russia (settlement, not origin).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Romani/Gypsies: originated from NW India (Punjab-Sindh region)
     - Word 'gypsy' = misnomer from 'Egyptian'
     - Romani language related to Sanskrit/Hindi
     - Genetic studies confirm South Asian origin
