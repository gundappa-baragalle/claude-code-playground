### 🟡 MEDIUM Priority — Full Options

**[Q4204]** [2021]
> Consider the following statements in respect of Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards:  1. Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are titles under the Article 18(1) of the Constitution of India. 2. Padma Awards, which were instituted in the year 1954, were suspended only once. 3. The number of Bharat Ratna Awards is restricted to a maximum of five in a particular year.  Which of the above statements are not correct?

     A) 1 and 2 only
     B) 2 and 3 only
     C) 1 and 3 only
  ✅ D) 1, 2 and 3

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may incorrectly assume Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are titles (they are NOT under Article 18(1)); Padma Awards were actually suspended twice, not once; Bharat Ratna has no fixed maximum per year.
  💡 **Eliminate:** All three statements are incorrect: these are not 'titles' under Article 18(1); Padma Awards were suspended twice (1977 and 1992); Bharat Ratna has no numerical cap per year.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Bharat Ratna and Padma Awards are NOT titles under Article 18(1) — they are civilian honours
     - Padma Awards were suspended twice: 1977-80 and 1992
     - No fixed maximum for Bharat Ratna in a year (though typically ≤3)

**[Q936]** [2001]
> Which of the following pairs is NOT correctly matched ?

     A) India’s first Technicolour film ... Jhansi Ki Rani
     B) India’s first 3-D film ... My Dear Kuttichat
     C) India’s first insured film ... Taal
  ✅ D) India’s first actress to win the Bharat Ratna ... Meena Kumari

  ⚠️ **Trap:** The question asks for the NOT correctly matched pair; students may not know that Meena Kumari never received the Bharat Ratna (she died in 1972 and was never awarded it); Lata Mangeshkar received it in 2001.
  💡 **Eliminate:** The first actress to win Bharat Ratna was actually never an actress in classical sense — Bharat Ratna went to M.S. Subbulakshmi (1998), not a film actress; Meena Kumari never received it — option D is incorrectly matched.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - No film actress named 'Meena Kumari' won Bharat Ratna; M.S. Subbulakshmi was the first woman musician to get it
     - India's first insured film was 'Taal' (1999) — this fact in option C is correct

### 🟢 LOW Priority — Full Options

**[Q1601]** [2005]
> Who among the following is not a recipient of the Bharat Ratna Award?

     A) Ustad Bismillah Khan
     B) Satyajit Ray
     C) Lata Mangeshkar
  ✅ D) Raj Kapoor

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Raj Kapoor is a celebrated Bollywood icon and may seem worthy of Bharat Ratna; students may confuse Dadasaheb Phalke Award recipients with Bharat Ratna awardees.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Ustad Bismillah Khan (2001), Satyajit Ray (1992), and Lata Mangeshkar (2001) are verified Bharat Ratna recipients; Raj Kapoor received Dadasaheb Phalke Award, not Bharat Ratna.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Raj Kapoor received the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 1987, not Bharat Ratna
     - Lata Mangeshkar received Bharat Ratna in 2001

**[Q581]** [1998]
> Bharat Ratna was awarded in 1997 to

     A) Dr. Homi Bhabha (Posthumous)
     B) Former President R. Venkataraman
     C) Satyajit Ray (Posthumous)
  ✅ D) Dr. Abdul Kalam

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Satyajit Ray received Bharat Ratna in 1992 (posthumous). Dr. Abdul Kalam received it in 1997. Homi Bhabha received it in 1966. Candidates may confuse years of award.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Bharat Ratna 1997 was awarded to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (then Principal Scientific Adviser). Satyajit Ray received it in 1992. R. Venkataraman did not receive Bharat Ratna. Answer D.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam received Bharat Ratna in 1997 for his contribution to India's missile and space programs
     - Satyajit Ray received Bharat Ratna in 1992
