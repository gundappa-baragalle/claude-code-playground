**[Q4709]** [2024]
> With reference to the Indian economy, "Collateral Borrowing and Lending Obligations" are the instruments of:

     A) Bond market
     B) Forex market
  ✅ C) Money market
     D) Stock market

  ⚠️ **Trap:** CBLO (Collateral Borrowing and Lending Obligations) sounds like it could be a bond or forex market instrument; it is a short-term collateralized borrowing instrument — a money market instrument.
  💡 **Eliminate:** CBLO (now replaced by TREPS — Tri-Party Repo) is a short-term secured borrowing instrument used in the overnight/short-term money market. Answer: C (Money market).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - CBLO (Collateral Borrowing and Lending Obligation): overnight/short-term money market instrument backed by government securities
     - Now superseded by Tri-Party Repo (TREPS) on CCIL; used by mutual funds, insurance companies for short-term liquidity
