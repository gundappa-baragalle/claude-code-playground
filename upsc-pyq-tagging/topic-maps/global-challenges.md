**[Q4380]** [2022]

     A) 1 and 2 only
     B) 2 and 3 only
     C) 1 and 3 only
  ✅ D) 1, 2 and 3

  ⚠️ **Trap:** All three UNCLOS provisions are standard and well-established; this question tests recall of specific UNCLOS articles rather than conceptual confusion.

Some of the International funding agencies have special terms for economic participation stipulating a substantial component of the aid to be used for sourcing equipment from the leading countries.
