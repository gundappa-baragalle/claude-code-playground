**[Q4785]** [1996]
> Mouse is to cat as fly is to

     A) rat
     B) animal
  ✅ C) spider
     D) horse

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Rat is a distractor because rats are also preyed upon by cats. The key relationship is predator-prey: cat preys on mouse; spider preys on fly.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Identify the relationship: cat is the natural predator of mouse. Apply to fly: spider is the natural predator of fly. Eliminate rat (preys on nothing listed), animal (too broad), horse (no predator relation to fly).
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Spider is the natural predator of fly — classic predator-prey analogy
     - Analogy questions test relational thinking, not factual knowledge

**[Q2326]** [2009] How many letters of the English alphabet (capitals) appear same when looked at in a mirror ?

     A) 9
     B) 10
  ✅ C) 11
     D) 12

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Students may not systematically identify all symmetric capital letters; the 11 are: A, H, I, M, O, T, U, V, W, X, Y — those with vertical axis of symmetry.

**[Q4638]** [1998] Which one of the following satisfies the relationship D d a : a D D : : R r b : ?

     A) D D A
     B) R R R
  ✅ C) b R R
     D) B B r

  ⚠️ **Trap:** The pattern involves case-shifting and positional logic; candidates may miss the structural symmetry between the two halves of the analogy.
