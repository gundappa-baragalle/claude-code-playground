**[Q1048]** [2002]
> Match List I (Ethnic Community) with List II (Country ) and select the correct answer using the codes given below the lists (Ethnic community) | (Country) A. Apatani | 1. China B. Dayak | 2. India C. Dinka | 3. Indonesia D. Uighur | 4. Sudan

  ✅ A) A-2, B-3, C-4, D-1
     B) A-3, B-2, C-4, D-1
     C) A-2, B-3, C-1, D-4
     D) A-3, B-2, C-1, D-4

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Dayak people live in Borneo which spans both Indonesia and Malaysia; Uighur-China link is well-known but some confuse them with Central Asian countries. Dinka-Sudan link is confused with Ethiopia.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Lock Uighur=China (highly newsworthy) and Dinka=Sudan/South Sudan first. Apatani are from Arunachal Pradesh (India). Dayak are from Borneo=Indonesia. Use elimination once 2-3 are confirmed.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Apatani: Arunachal Pradesh, India — known for facial tattoos
     - Dayak: Borneo/Indonesia — sea-faring indigenous
     - Dinka: South Sudan — largest ethnic group there
     - Uighur: Xinjiang, China — Turkic Muslim minority

**[Q660]** [1999]
> A person of mixed European and Indian blood in Latin America is called a

     A) Mulatto
  ✅ B) Mestizo
     C) Meiji
     D) Mau Mau

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Mulatto (African-European mix) sounds similar to Mestizo; Meiji is associated with Japanese emperor/era creating confusion; Mau Mau is a Kenyan nationalist movement.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Mestizo = mixed European and indigenous (Native American) ancestry in Latin America; Mulatto = mixed African and European; these are distinct racial terms.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Mestizo: European + Indigenous American ancestry — majority population in many Latin American countries
     - Mulatto: European + African ancestry — common in Brazil, Caribbean

**[Q372]** [1997]
> The tribal population in Andaman and Nicobar Islands belongs to the

     A) Australoid race
     B) Caucasoid race
     C) Mongoloid race
  ✅ D) Negroid race

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Mongoloid race may seem correct given proximity to Southeast Asia; Australoid is often confused with Australian Aborigines rather than Andaman tribes.
  💡 **Eliminate:** The Andamanese (Jarawa, Sentinelese, Onge, Great Andamanese) are classified as Negrito/Negroid race — among the oldest surviving human populations.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Andaman tribes (Jarawa, Sentinelese, Onge) belong to Negrito racial group
     - Negritos are among the earliest Homo sapiens to migrate out of Africa

### 🟢 LOW Priority — Full Options

**[Q704]** [1999]
> Which one of the following pairs of states and tribes is not correctly matched?

     A) Assam : Miri
     B) Nagaland : Konyak
     C) Aranachal Pradesh : Apatani
  ✅ D) Madhya Pradesh : Lambada

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Lambada tribe is commonly associated with Andhra Pradesh/Telangana — assigning it to Madhya Pradesh is the deliberate mismatch in option D.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Lambada (Banjara) tribe is found in Andhra Pradesh/Telangana, not Madhya Pradesh — D is the incorrect pair; Miri (Assam), Konyak (Nagaland), Apatani (Arunachal) are correctly matched.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Lambada (Banjara) tribe is associated with Andhra Pradesh/Telangana, not Madhya Pradesh
     - Apatani tribe of Arunachal Pradesh is known for distinctive facial tattoos
