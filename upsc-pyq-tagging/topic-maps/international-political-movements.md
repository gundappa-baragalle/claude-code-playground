**[Q2830]** [2014]
> Recently, a series of uprisings of people referred to as 'Arab Spring' originally started from

     A) Egypt
     B) Lebanon
     C) Syria
  ✅ D) Tunisia

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Egypt had the most prominent Arab Spring moment with Tahrir Square, making it a common wrong answer; Arab Spring actually started in Tunisia with the self-immolation of Mohamed Bouazizi.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Arab Spring began in Tunisia in December 2010 with Mohamed Bouazizi's self-immolation triggering mass protests; spread to Egypt, Libya, Syria — answer is D.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Arab Spring started in Tunisia, December 2010 (Mohamed Bouazizi self-immolation)
     - Tunisia was the only country where Arab Spring led to successful democratic transition

**[Q1282]** [2003]
> The Basque people who are frequently in the news for their separatist activity live in

     A) France
     B) Italy
     C) Portugal
  ✅ D) Spain

  ⚠️ **Trap:** France also has a significant Basque population (in the Pyrénées-Atlantiques region), so France is a plausible distractor. However, the main separatist movement ETA was based in Spanish Basque Country.
  💡 **Eliminate:** The Basque separatist movement (ETA) is primarily associated with Spain (Basque Country: Euskadi). While Basques also live in France, the separatist violence was centered in Spain. Answer: D.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Basque people: Spain (primarily) and France (lesser extent)
     - ETA (Euskadi Ta Askatasuna): Basque separatist organization in Spain
     - ETA declared permanent ceasefire 2011, disarmed 2017

**[Q2]** [1995]
> Which of the following are correctly matched ? I. Hamas—Palestinian extremist group II. Sinn Fein—IRA’s political wing III. True Path Party—A major constituent of the ruling coalition in Turkey Choose the correct answer from the codes given below : Codes :

     A) I and II
     B) I and III
  ✅ C) I, II and III
     D) II and III

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Sinn Féin–IRA link is often questioned (some say Sinn Féin is separate); True Path Party (DYP) of Turkey is obscure — students may confuse it with AKP or MHP.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Hamas as Palestinian group ✓. Sinn Féin as IRA's political wing ✓ (historically correct pre-Good Friday). True Path Party was indeed in Turkish coalition (Çiller govt 1990s) ✓. All three correct → C.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Hamas: Islamist Palestinian movement, founded 1987
     - Sinn Féin: political wing of IRA (pre-Good Friday Agreement 1998)
     - True Path Party (DYP): centre-right Turkish party, PM Tansu Çiller's coalition in 1990s

**[Q2237]** [2009]
> In the latter half of the year 2008, which one of the following countries pulled out of Commonwealth of Independent States ?

     A) Azerbaijan
     B) Belarus
  ✅ C) Georgia
     D) Kazakhstan

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Belarus or Kazakhstan might seem likely given post-Soviet tensions; Georgia pulled out of CIS after the Russia-Georgia war of August 2008 over South Ossetia.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Georgia withdrew from CIS in 2008 following the Russia-Georgia war over South Ossetia; withdrawal effective August 2009 — answer is C.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Georgia withdrew from CIS in 2008 after Russia-Georgia war over South Ossetia
     - Ukraine later also left CIS effectively after 2014 Crimea annexation

**[Q1028]** [2001]
> Falun Gong is

     A) an ethnic minority in Eastern China
     B) an insurgency outfit in Western China
     C) a pro-democracy movement in China
  ✅ D) a spiritual movement in China

  ⚠️ **Trap:** Options A and B describe it as an ethnic or insurgency movement; Option C (pro-democracy) is partially true as adherents faced persecution, but the movement is primarily spiritual/religious, not a political democracy movement.
  💡 **Eliminate:** Falun Gong (also Falun Dafa) is a spiritual movement founded in China in 1992 combining meditation and moral teachings; banned by China in 1999. Answer D: spiritual movement.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - Falun Gong is a Chinese spiritual practice founded by Li Hongzhi in 1992 based on qigong and Buddhist principles
     - Banned in China in 1999; practitioners face persecution; the movement has millions of adherents worldwide

**[Q269]** [1996]
> In the 1996 Cricket World Cup, the non-test playing countries which participated included

     A) UAE, Kenya and Canada
     B) UAE, Kenya and Hong Kong
  ✅ C) UAE, Kenya and Holland
     D) Canada, Kenya and Hong Kong

  ⚠️ **Trap:** This is a very specific 1996 current affairs question; UAE, Kenya, and Holland (Netherlands) were the non-test playing countries in the 1996 Cricket World Cup.
  💡 **Eliminate:** 1996 World Cup (India-Pakistan-Sri Lanka): non-Test nations included UAE, Kenya, and Holland — answer is C.
  📌 **Key facts:**
     - 1996 Cricket World Cup: UAE, Kenya, Holland were non-Test playing associate nations
     - Kenya famously defeated West Indies in 1996 World Cup
